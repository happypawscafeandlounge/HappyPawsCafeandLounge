Happy Paws Cafe and Lounge

"Like the cat that got the cream"

Discover the enchanting world of our cat cafe, where the cozy aroma of freshly brewed beverages meets the playful purrs of our delightful feline companions. Nestled in the heart of Batangas, our carefully curated space invites you to unwind, sip on artisanal drinks, and enjoy the unique charm that our resident cats bring to your experience. Join us for a moment of warmth, connection, and the joy that only a cat-filled atmosphere can provide.

Let's explore...

Hi there! I'm Shiella Mae Aves Carreal, the proud owner and cat enthusiast behind Happy Paws Café and Lounge. Welcome to our cozy haven for cat lovers, where a cup of coffee comes with a side of purrs and playtime. Growing up, I always had a deep affinity for cats. Their playful antics and comforting purrs brought immense joy to my life. In 2023, I decided to turn my passion into something more, and that's when Happy Paws Café and Lounge was born.

Happy Paws Café and Lounge isn't just a cafe; it's a whisker wonderland. Our space is carefully designed to provide a serene environment for both humans and cats to coexist harmoniously. From cozy nooks for napping to interactive play zones, every inch is crafted with love.

About our cafe

Happy Paws Café and Lounge aims to rescue and adapt stray cats. Also, they will promote rescue cats that are available for adoption to give them the love and care they deserve. Moreover, this cat café also aims to create stress-free, tranquil environments where people and cats can relax next to one another. Happy Paws Café and Lounge can help its customers gain new insight, present themselves, and refresh and repair their broken souls.

In essence, we are fostering a symbiotic relationship between humans and cats by exhibiting and teaching the best standards of cat care and facilitating access to animal assisted therapy for those suffering from depression and other mental illness. At Happy Paws Café and Lounge, our commitment goes beyond creating a delightful space for cat lovers. We are on a mission to make a positive impact on the lives of stray and rescue cats.

About our cafe

Our cat cafe serves as a haven for these furry friends, offering them a second chance at a loving home. Our adoption program is at the heart of what we do. We actively work to rescue and rehabilitate stray cats, providing them with the care and attention they deserve. By promoting these rescue cats for adoption, we strive to connect them with compassionate individuals who can offer them a forever home. Every adoption not only transforms the life of a cat but also enriches the lives of those who open their hearts to these wonderful companions.

Beyond our dedication to feline welfare, we aspire to create stress-free and tranquil environments. Happy Paws Café and Lounge is designed to be a sanctuary where people and cats can find solace together. We believe in the therapeutic power of the human-animal bond, and our space is carefully curated to facilitate moments of relaxation and joy for both our visitors and our resident cats.

About our cafe

At Happy Paws Café and Lounge, we take pride in offering a diverse menu to cater to every taste bud. From the comforting warmth of hot drinks, including rich espressos, soothing teas, and decadent hot chocolates, to the refreshing coolness of iced coffees, teas, and chilled fruit blends, we have a beverage for every mood and season.

For those with a sweet tooth, our menu boasts a tempting selection of desserts and pastries. Indulge in scrumptious cakes, cookies, flaky pastries, and creamy delights, each crafted to satisfy your cravings and elevate your culinary experience.
